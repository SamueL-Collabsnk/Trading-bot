## 🚀 HOW TO USE RIGHT NOW

### Step 1: Open Browser

Go to: **http://localhost:8501**

### Step 2: Pick a Market

1. Click **📂 Select Market Category** dropdown
2. Choose: 💰 💶 💷 💵 🪙 or 📊
3. Click **📌 Select Trading Symbol**
4. Pick your instrument (XAUUSD, EURUSD, BTCUSD, etc.)

### Step 3: Watch Chart Update

- Green candles 🟩 = Price UP
- Red candles 🟥 = Price DOWN
- Blue line = Trend direction
- 4 metrics = Price range & volatility

### Step 4: See Bot Analysis

- What's the market regime?
- What signals are generated?
- Is it safe? (Risk level)
- What does AI explain?

### Step 5: Make Decision

- ✅ **APPROVE** the bot's signal
- ❌ **REJECT** if you disagree
- ⏸️ **DEFER** to wait
- ✏️ **MODIFY** if needed

### Step 6: (Optional) Refresh

- Click 🔄 button to get latest data instantly

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Q: Chart not showing?**
A: Click 🔄 Refresh, wait 5s, try EURUSD

**Q: Dashboard slow?**
A: Click Refresh, close extra tabs, try different symbol

**Q: "No data" message?**
A: Check market hours, try EURUSD, verify internet

**Q: Bot not analyzing?**
A: Click Refresh, wait 10s, try EURUSD

**Q: Port 8501 in use?**
A: Run `pkill -f streamlit` then restart
