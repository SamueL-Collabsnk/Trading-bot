   print(f"AUC: {metrics['mean_auc']:.3f}")
   EOF

⚠️  IMPORTANT NOTES

1. MetaTrader5 (MT5)
   └─ Windows-only API
   └─ On Linux: Use CCXT for crypto trading instead

2. XGBoost
   └─ Skipped due to disk space (CUDA packages)
   └─ LightGBM (✓ installed) is primary model
   └─ sklearn.GradientBoosting available as fallback

3. Model Training
   └─ Walk-forward CV prevents overfitting
   └─ Minimum 500 bars recommended
   └─ Auto-retrains every 24 hours

4. Signal Quality
   └─ Backtest ≥1000 signals before deployment
   └─ Minimum 50% win rate recommended
   └─ Average R:R should be ≥2.0

5. Risk Management
   └─ Always use stop-loss
   └─ Position size based on risk percentage
   └─ Never risk more than 1-2% per trade
