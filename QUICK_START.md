## 🎮 How to Use

### **Step 1: Change Market**

1. Click dropdown **📂 Select Market Category**
2. Choose one:
   - **💰 Precious Metals** → XAUUSD, XAGUSD
   - **💶 EUR Pairs** → EURUSD, EURGBP, etc.
   - **💷 GBP Pairs** → GBPUSD, GBPJPY, etc.
   - **💵 USD Pairs** → USDJPY, USDCAD, etc.
   - **🪙 Cryptocurrencies** → BTCUSD, ETHUSD
   - **📊 Other Metals** → XPDUSD, XPTUSD

### **Step 2: Pick a Symbol**

3. Click dropdown **📌 Select Trading Symbol**
4. Choose your instrument (e.g., EURUSD, XAUUSD)
5. **Chart & analysis auto-update! ✨**

### **Step 3: Analyze the Chart**

6. **Green candles** 🟩 = Price going UP
7. **Red candles** 🟥 = Price going DOWN
8. **Blue line** = Trend direction
9. Check **4 metrics** for price range

### **Step 4: Read the Signals**

10. See what the bot says under "Strategy Signals"
11. Check if it's safe in "Risk Assessment"
12. Read the AI explanation

### **Step 5: Make Your Call**

13. Click **✅ APPROVE** to accept bot's signal
14. Click **❌ REJECT** to disagree
15. Click **⏸️ DEFER** to wait for more data
16. Click **✏️ MODIFY** to customize

### **Step 6: Refresh (Optional)**

17. Click **🔄 Refresh** to update instantly
    - Clears cache
    - Gets latest data
    - Re-runs analysis
